Understanding IVF

In Vitro Fertilization (IVF) is a fertility treatment where eggs are combined with sperm outside the body in a specialized laboratory. Once embryos are formed, they are monitored for 3-5 days before the healthiest one is gently transferred into the uterus.

It is often the most effective solution for:

  • Blocked Fallopian Tubes
  • Endometriosis
  • Male Factor Infertility
  • Unexplained Infertility

The Journey

Your Roadmap to Parenthood

01

Stimulation

Daily injections for 10-12 days to stimulate ovaries to produce multiple eggs.

02

Egg Retrieval

A short, painless procedure under sedation to collect the eggs.

03

Fertilization

Eggs and sperm are combined in the lab. We use ICSI for higher fertilization rates.

04

Transfer

The best embryo is placed into the uterus. A simple procedure needing no anesthesia.
