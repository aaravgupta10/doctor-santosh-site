Fertility Enhancing Surgery

Laparoscopy & Hysteroscopy

Minimally invasive "keyhole" procedures to diagnose and correct structural issues affecting fertility, ensuring a healthy environment for conception.

Precision Surgery

Sometimes, the barrier to pregnancy is physical. Laparoscopy and Hysteroscopy are advanced, minimally invasive surgical techniques that allow Dr. Santosh Gupta to see inside the reproductive organs and correct problems in the same sitting.

These procedures are the gold standard for treating:

  • Uterine Fibroids & Polyps
  • Ovarian Cysts (PCOS Drilling, Endometriomas)
  • Blocked Fallopian Tubes
  • Uterine Septum or Adhesions

The Procedures

Two Complementary Techniques

Hysteroscopy

A thin, lighted telescope is passed through the cervix into the uterus. No cuts are made on the skin.

  • Removes polyps & small fibroids
  • Corrects uterine septum
  • Evaluates uterine lining

Laparoscopy

A "keyhole" surgery where a camera is inserted through a tiny incision in the navel to view the pelvic area.

  • Treats Endometriosis
  • Removes Ovarian Cysts
  • Checks Tubal Patency (Dye Test)

Benefits of Minimally Invasive Surgery

Faster Recovery

Most patients are discharged on the same day (Day Care Procedure) and return to work within 2-3 days.

Less Pain & Scarring

Tiny incisions mean significantly less postoperative pain and minimal cosmetic scarring compared to open surgery.

Improved Conception Rates

Correcting structural defects like a septum or fibroids often leads to spontaneous pregnancy or higher IVF success.
