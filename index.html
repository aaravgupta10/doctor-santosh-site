Meet Your Specialist

Dr. Santosh Gupta Senior IVF Specialist in Koramangala & Bannerghatta

Infertility is an emotional journey that requires expert guidance. Dr. Santosh Gupta combines advanced IVF & ICSI protocols with compassionate care to help couples in Koramangala and Bannerghatta Road, Bengaluru achieving their dream of parenthood.

With over 25 years of experience in reproductive medicine, she specializes in treating complex cases including PCOS, Endometriosis, Male Infertility, and Recurrent Pregnancy Loss. Her approach prioritizes ethics, transparency, and customized treatment plans for higher success rates.
